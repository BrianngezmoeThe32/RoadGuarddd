## How to Use the Motor Service App

This app allows users to request on-demand motor services such as fuel delivery, tire change, towing, and other roadside assistance — anytime, anywhere.

---

### 1. Creating an Account
- Open the app and tap *Sign Up*.
- Enter your full name, email, phone number, and create a password.
- Verify your account via the email or SMS confirmation (if required).
- Once done, you’ll be redirected to the *Login* screen.

---

### 2. Logging In
- On the *Login* screen, enter your registered email and password.
- Tap *Login* to access your account.
- If you forgot your password, use the *Forgot Password* option to reset it.

---

### 3. Setting Up Your Profile
- After logging in, go to your *Profile* section.
- Add important details such as:
  - Vehicle type (e.g., car, bakkie, motorcycle)
  - Registration number
  - Preferred payment method
- Save your profile — this helps service providers assist you faster.

---

### 4. Requesting a Service
- Tap *Request Service* on the main menu.
- Choose the service you need:
  - Fuel Delivery  
  - Tire Change  
  - Towing Service  
  - Battery Jump Start  
- Allow location access so the app can find your position.
- Confirm your request — the nearest available service provider will be assigned.

---

### 5. Tracking and Communication
- You can view the provider’s location in real time on the map.
- Chat or call the provider directly for updates or clarification.

---

### 6. Payment and Completion
- Once the service is complete, you’ll receive a summary with the cost.
- Choose your payment option (cash, card, or in-app payment).
- Rate the service provider to help improve future experiences.

---

### 7. Logging Out
- Go to the *Profile* menu.
- Tap *Log Out* to safely exit your account.
